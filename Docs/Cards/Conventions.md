# Card Design Writing Conventions

## Shorthands:
### Energy Cost
Fire (F), Earth (E), Water (W), Air (A), Generic (#)
  - e.g. 2 Generic, 3 Fire, 1 Water = {2FFFW}

### Rarity
Common (C), Uncommon (U), Rare (R), Legendary 

## Card Template:

\<Name\> (\<Rarity\>): \{\<Energy Cost\>\}
- List of Effects

e.g. 
Steam Attack (C): {1FW}
- Deal 8 damage
- Draw a card
- Gain {FW}
